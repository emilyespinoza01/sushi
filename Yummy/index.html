About Us

Sushi Whirl is a top-rated sushi restaurant located in Atlanta, GA. Our innovative conveyor system seamlessly delivers fresh and delectable sushi right to your table, ensuring a dining adventure like no other.

We offer a wide variety of sushi options, including classic rolls, sashimi, and nigiri, as well as unique specialties. With friendly service, affordable prices, and a fun atmosphere. Sushi Whirl is the perfect place to satisfy your sushi cravings.

Come visit us today and see why we're the top choice for sushi lovers in Atlanta!

Why Choose Sushi Whirl?

At our restaurant, we offer a truly unique dining experience. Our conveyor belt system allows you to select freshly made sushi as it passes by your table, providing a dynamic and interactive dining experience unlike any other.

Sushi Whirl 101

Our staff will guide you to your table by the sushi conveyor belt! You can start picking sushi plates right away. Use the tablet to order drinks, appetizers, and special items. Bella the Robot will swiftly deliver your drinks and wasabi plates.

Select sushi plates from the conveyor belt as they come by. Take the whole plate. Don't grab sushi from moving plates. Once you take a plate, please don't put it back or return empty plates. Stack your empty plates on the table when you're done.

Use the tablet to order what you want—drinks, appetizers, hand rolls, desserts, and more! Just a few taps and your order will be brought to your table by Bella or the Sushi Factory Trolley.

Tap the “Bill” button when you are finished with your meal. We will bring your bill to you.
